Magic

Aasimar Lineage

All bodily magic is channeled through Aasimar blood in Orbis. The Faith preaches that when the Last Empereor defeated the Malum and subsiquently obtained the power of the Gods, he gave his people, the Orions, the power of magic through their bloodlines. As such even lessor species that contain drops of the Aasimar bloodline can produce magical effects. These species are often persicuted for using magic.

Soul Stone Properties

All magical items use soul stones for sources of power. These stones are charged via killing, or when the Storm of Souls is ravaging the land.

  • Amethysts - Flow. Amethysts are stones that are used to augment spiritual energy and allow conduits to conduct souls better.

    •  

  • Diamond - Awe. Diamonds are used to charm and magically influence the actions of others.

    •  

  • Emerald - Decay/Rot. Emeralds harness the power of decay to collect life energy quicker and easier.

    •  

  • Pearl - Floatation. Pearls allow for floatation in water and flight in the air.

    •  

  • Ruby - Destruction. Rubies harness destructive powers of the elements to create elements in weapons or out of stones.

    •  

  • Sapphire - Restoration. Sapphires are stones of regeneration and allow for quick or delayed covent activiations.

    •  

  • Opal - Creation. Opals allow for the augemention and awakening of powers and abilities artificially.

    •  

  • Topaz - Protection. Topazs work best in armors, and create a magical shield that lessens impacts on the being.

    •  

  • Onyx - Death. Onyx are sealers of death and curses.
