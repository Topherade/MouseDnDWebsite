Performance Skills

1. Astrology Once / council member - If asked, tell a player their sign - flip a coin, heads +2 PAR, tails, -2 PAR
2. Ceremony Once - members of your religion gain +1 PAR, non-members lose 1 PAR
3. Comedian Once - Party member with lowest int gains +2 PAR
4. Dancing Green Card - Shift when an adjacent opponent shifts
5. Debate Daily - For 1 hour target loses 2 PAR. Enemies affected by this cannot engage you.
6. Dream Interpretation Daily - Send dm a "dream" message during night phase, it is forwarded to a player (May affect PAR)
7. Duelling Yellow Card - (LA) Only one opponent can attack you this turn
8. Fast Talking Daily - Gain 10xLuck gold from any gold related transaction
9. Heraldry Once / house - +3 influence for any house you herald
10. Hypnotism Daily - Add your PAR score to another mouse's ability roll
11. Iaijutsu Yellow Card - (LA) Pull out katana and attack
12. Juggling Can carry one more item than you can convince the DM you are carrying
13. Magician Once - Use telekinesis to manipulate up to 10 lbs within 30 feet. Lasts 1 minute.
14. Medium Once - Gain contact with one dead family member, gain all of their feats. They can answer one question.
15. Play Instrument Once - make a song about a player; they gain a feat / quirk
16. Noh If you are a farmer, become a military leader during adventure (in addition to farmer)
17. Omen Interpretation Once - Force one mouse to gain +/- 2 PAR (Random; both mice gain plot information in secret)
18. Oratory Gain diplomacy or persuasion, or a random skill if both are already aquired
19. Origami Gain slight of hand skill, or a random skill if already aquired
20. Painting Once - +10 influence with a family
21. Poetry Gain a free spouse from a mouse house
22. Prestidigitation Daily - Can start a fire from apparently nothing
23. Prophecy Choose one child's profession and highest stat (if you survive to have children)
24. Rhetoric Daily - gain +2 on all CHA checks with target non-hostile mouse
25. Sketching Gain +5 to investigation checks to identify any object / creature you have sketched (Must have appearance described)
26. Shamanistic Ritual Once - A dying mouse lives for one extra day, and gets a make all checks to recover normally for that day
27. Singing +25 gold per marriage ceremony
28. Soothsaying Once - During night, tell DM the next day's weather
29. Storytelling Tell a deed about a non-surviving house's mouse (+15 influence for you if good, -5 to them with all other houses if bad
30. Thaumaturgy Once per mouse - Create enough cheese (out of rocks) to feed your party
31. Tightrope Walking +1 AGL, lost if you lose a leg
32. Trance Injuries don't affect you; Additional damage to damaged body parts at end of combat
33. Ventriloquism Once per mouse - you can say anything and all other mice think target mouse said that thing; Gain mimicry
34. Weapon Finesse Green Card - Minor victory becomes major victory (before damage location is determined)
35. Whistling / Humming Daily - If lost, even if your team isn't looking for you, you are found
36. Weaving Daily - repair one cloth / fiber / leather item.
