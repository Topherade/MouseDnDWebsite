    • History of The Wain

    The Wain have been a proud family of brewers and wine makers for as long as mice can remember. They are a very fickle group, only worshipping Soloensis as they believe it benefits them with trade. They often do not involve themselves in wars that other Daimyos fight, and follow who ever is in control of Edo.
