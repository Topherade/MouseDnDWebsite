  • Chujitsuna Genji - Lead the Genji during the Hijo Rebellion. He was the last in a long string of heirs that were heavly involved with booze and whores. The Hijo rose up against this incompatancy and started a rebellion in 901AT.

    • History of The Hijo

    The Genji Shogunate fought the Hijo Daiymo and their allies in 901 AT. Moving from Aokigahara the Hijo cut off the Genji supply line to Edo and sieged it out until they gain control of the Fort. They then seized the emperior in Kyo in 906 AT. The Genji being starved out on all sides after the Kirikabu of Cyprus Grove betrayed them in 907AT surrendered unconditionally in 908 AT.

    The Hijo replaced the leaders of the Genji with some lesser branch of the house, forcing them to marry Hijo Mice to maintain relations with Hijo.
