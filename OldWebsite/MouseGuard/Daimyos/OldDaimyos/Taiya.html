  • Tokimasa Hijo - Tokimasa lead the Hijo Daimyo to victory against the former Genji Shogunate with his brother Takatoki Hijo in 908 AT. He then established the Hijo Council that meets every 4 moons to discuss current events and to partake in current issues that face the Hojo realm.

    • History of The Hijo

    The Hijo rose to powerin the late 800's. Once they obtained significant support from other Daimyos, they fought the Genji Shogunate in 901 AT. Moving from Aokigahara they cut off the Genji supply line to Edo and sieged it out until they gain control of the Fort. They then seized the emperior in Kyo in 906 AT. The Genji being starved out on all sides after the Kirikabu of Cyprus Grove betrayed them in 907 surrendered unconditionally in 908 AT.

    Once they have gained control, they created the Hijo shogunate. Every four moons the Hijo Council meets. The Hijo Council is a meeting of delegates from all the Daimyos in Hijo. Through this Council, the Hijo solve more intense problems in various regions of Hojo. The 908 Council snuffed out resistance. The 912 Council fought off a weasal incursion in Cyprus Grove.
