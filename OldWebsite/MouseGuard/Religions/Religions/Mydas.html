    • About Mydas

    Mydas is the guardian spirit of the Floating Mountain. She is a SeaTurtle Spirit whom embodies intelligence and sloth. Favor with Mydas is gained through feats of intelligence in her name, as well as offerings in her name. Examples of these include worshipping at her mountain, writing great works in her name, braving the seas, praying everyday in the direction of her mountain or offerings of the brains of mice. Commune can be reached with Mydas at the Floating Mountain Shrine.

    Mydas has long sense been the guardian spirit of the seas. Most of the sea faring clans worship her and as such benefit from her domain. The Umigame are adviced by an ancient sea turtle as well as a tortoise who act as a conduit for Mydas. The Tokage build their homes in the shells of dead turtles after the blessing of Mydas. This usually requires an offering of mice brains.

    In 950 AT when Yogoroth released the Pillars of Hojo, the floating mountain sank. With his home gone, Mydas promised his followers he would return and fled the area.
