    • About Komodoenisis

    Komodeonsis is the guardian spirit of the Immortal Mountain. He is a Giant Lizard Spirit whom embodies strength and gluttony. Favor with Komodeonsis is gained through feats of strength in his name, as well as sacrifices in his name. Examples of these include climbing his mountain to his shrine on the top, constructing monuments in his name, conquering other daimyos, sacrificing food, or offerings of the stomachs of mice. Commune can be reached with Komodeonsis at Taisha Shrine, or at his mountain top shrine.

    Personality

    Gluttonous

    History

    After Hijo gain control of Hojo in 908 from the Genji family, Tokimasa Hijo give control of the Aokigahara region to his younger brother Takatoki Hijo. Takatoki then married into the Taisha clan and took their name and converted to Komodeonsis. Hijo also converted, as well as forcing the Emperior's house Tobo to convert.

    The Weishu clan under Wei Mingxing forcefully took the Hijo's blood stone, and summoned Komodoenisis at the burning of Edo in 936 AT. This Weishu clan used his might to sack the Tobo's forbiden fortress and marry into their lineage for the bloodline power. The Weishu then went through a religious purging of Hojo elimating the other, old spirits, and installing a single religion with a pantheon of spirits.

    Komodoenisis became the king of spirits, ruling over all others from his Immortal Mountain. The new spirits of Hojo, Kumogataru and Yogoroth, pay tribute to his might.
