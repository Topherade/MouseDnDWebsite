    Ursine is the guardian spirit of the forgotten mountain. He was a long lost deity from a forgotten age, his temple was unearthed by the wandering pilgrims of heartbreaker bog when they made his mountain there home. Favor is earned by feats of strength, respects characters with strong will, as well as unquestioning loyalty.

    Ursine was originally worshiped by the people of the desert nation of Sabaku, its people disappeared before recorded history and were unknown until the pilgrims of heartbreaker bogdiscovered an ancient lost city in the forgotten mountain, now known as Kuma. Within they revealed the slumbering bear spirit known as Ursine. Ursine radiates a trusting aura, as well as an intense strength. It is said that to be in his presence is to know the very essence of strength, both of mind and body. He bonded with the wayward mice of heartbreaker bog as they had survived a great plight and were searching for a new destiny. Ursine aided them in restoring Kuma to its former glory. He is as loyal to his subject’s as they are to him and he places high value into loyalty to one's Allies. The Himitsu mice are very secretive when it comes to their religion and don’t share this information with outsiders, and as of today the location of Ursine’s temple in the mountain remains a secret, known only by the royalty of Himitsu.
