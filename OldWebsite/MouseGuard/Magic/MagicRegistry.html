List of the Magical and Dangerious Items of Hojo

This List was created by The Izu Institution under the Law passed by the First NingyoMordla Legislation in 936 AT and has been appended multiple times contains the last known locations of the 9 Major Blood Stones, and other magical and dangerous items in the lands of Hojo.

Major Blood Stones

  • Amethyst - Unknown, could not be created, must currently exist somewhere.
  • Diamond - In the holy sceptar of the Weizhu Diamyo.
  • Emerald - In magical Dagger possesed by the Dreadwood Mercs.
  • Pearl - Was on the Floating mountain before it sank, unknown.
  • Ruby - Destroyed when it touched the Onyx stone. New Ruby stone was created in the Izu Institute which it currently possesses.
  • Sapphire - Unknown, was shattered by Kaito Mordla when he was brought back to life.
  • Opal - Was created by the Wei Diamyo and is now in the Izu Institution.
  • Topaz - In the head between the two picks of the Mordla Diamyo's Sacred Pickaxe.
  • Onyx - Held by the Wei Diamyo.

    Other Objects of Power

  • Wall Pillager - A large cannon that was mounted on the back of beatles by the Dreadwood Mercs. Is currently Mounted on the walls of Nakajima but does not have any an amunition.
