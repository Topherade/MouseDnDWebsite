Cheese and Item Magic

Summary

Often considered the lesser of the Three Magics, Cheese and Item Magic lumps together magic that uses items of neither blood or spiritual origin. As cheese is holy and great, it has healing properities. Other items are ancient artifacts that possess a power that The Institute is unable to understand. The subdisiplines in Cheese and Item Magic are Food Magic and Artificing.

 

Subdomains

Cheese/Food Magic

The favorite food of the races often has special healling properties. Cheese in general for mice can be used to fight off discesses and near death. It is often used as the cure all drug by the mice of Hojo and as such has great value.

 

Item Magic

Item magic is the use of ancient artificacts to create such things as lightning and fire from next to nothing, run great calculations in a mater of seconds, or communicate vast distances.
