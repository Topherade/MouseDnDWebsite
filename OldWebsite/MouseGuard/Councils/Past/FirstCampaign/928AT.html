Council of 928 AT

Political Shifts

  • The Morgulia (Willy), Weishu (Shane) and the Famu took over the East and West Kurobe Gorges.

    • Day 1 The Council Meeting - 13th Day of First Moon of Fall

  • The 928 AT council meet to discuss the rumors from the Gorges of a cat moving through the teritories. The council was dispatched to track and hunt the cat.

    • Day 2-11 Journey to HeartBreakerBog - 14th-24th Day of First Moon of Fall

  • The Merchant Group - The party meets a group of merchants from Mashita. They have slaves, an exotic cannon, and other various goods. The the Morgulia heir (willy) buys a squirel that was a slave. The HeartBreaker Bog (Joe) mouse bought a child slave (to be "adopted").

    • Day 12 The Party at HeartBreaker Bog - 25th Day of First Moon of Fall

  • The Heartbreaker Bog (Joe) invites The blue town mouse (Haley), The Rodes Mouse (Mitch), the Dreadwood Merc (Levi), the Morgula mouse (Willy), and the Weishu mouse (Shane) to a party at his castle. The Rodes mouse, Morgula mouse, and the Weishu mouse go out gambling, but only the Weishu mouse is successful. In the morning, the Weishu mouse finds his beatle missing, taken by two armed mice who claimed to be of the Weishu clan. The heartbreaker bog mouse sent a troop of grasshoppers after them while the party proceded after the cat.

    • Day 13-18 Following the Kitty - 25h-30th Day of First Moon of Fall

  • The Death of the Famu - During one of the nights that the party follows the cat's trail, the Famu is found to be dead by coughing up blood. The DreadWoodMerch discovers him, and feels sick. The Morgulia mouse (willy) saves the Dreadwood merc by feeding him cheese. The body, tent, and belongs of the Famu were all burned.
  • The finding of the old mouse - The group came across a sleeping old mouse in the middle of the cat's trail. He claims to be a Famu with a sheru coin. After discussing with the burned old mouse, the party discovers that he might be the mouse that was burned in the Decon swamps 8 moons ago. The old mouse then rallys other mice and the cat to attack the council. After a long battle, The cat and enemy mice are slain. In the battle, Levi who lead the charge was beheaded and slain first, The Wain, Taisha, and Taiya also lost their lives. Dealing the Killing blow to the cat, Alongside of Joe and Shane, Willy was slain. Mitch's squire was also killed.

    • Day 19-20 Return to Edo - 2nd Day of Second Moon of Fall

    The Offical Report to the Council

  • The Cat was slain
  • The council returned the possesions of the wain, taisha, taiya, Dreadwood, and Morgulia Clans.
  • The Famu died a Coward running from the cat.
