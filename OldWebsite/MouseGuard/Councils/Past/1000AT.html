Council of 908 AT

The 908AT Hijo council was the Hijo Council. After the Hijo had taken Edo, they set about creating this council so that problems of the realms could be solved fairly easily. The task of this first council was to deal with the rebels who still remained after the recent change in government. The Taisha, Hijo, Wain, Kirikabu, and the Taiya banded together to put down the past shogun the Minamoto.
