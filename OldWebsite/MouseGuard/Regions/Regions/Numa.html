The Land of Numa - Gathering Place of the Rohans

Government

  • The Council of Nine

    • Terrain

    Numa was founded by banished nobles and warriors from Hojo, Yamashan, and Haiwan. It maintains control of the contested region of Ronina through the power of the miltary it controls, as well as the fact that none of the countries near if find little value in the region. Numa is built into the roots of a large willow tree thats on a stream that leads into the Decon Swamps.

    • Areas of Note

  • Numa - Numa is built into the roots of a large willow tree thats on a stream that leads into the Decon Swamps. The city is rulled by the Council of Nine, The Nine major industries of Numa; Mining, Logging, Woodworking, Stoneworking, Blacksmithing, Medicine, Merchants, and Mercenaries. The council hall is built inside of the great Willow that guards Numa. Here the mice decide how to best govern Ronina and to decide to deal with or not to deal with threats in areas of the region.

  • Torera - A great metal fort that was reclaimed from the Decon Swamps in 958 AT after Numa joined Hojo. The Fort has been purged of the mice that had lived there. Sometimes medicine is found deep in the walls of the fort. Torera is know for the Medicine it produces, as well as the black market goods such as drugs and slaves it sells.

  • Kinoko Grove - A forest of Mushroom Trees. Two kinds of Mushrooms grow here, GrenKo, Halucinagenic Green shrooms, and PurpKo, Poisonous Purple shrooms. Both are restricted in Hojo without a license.
