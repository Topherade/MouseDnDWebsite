    • Terrain

    The Capital providence of the Hojo region consists of a very large fortress with a little farmland around it. This fortress has three levels, each of which extends 100s of mice up, along with a large roof access. The ground inside the fort varies from a wooly feeling, stone, or wood depending on which area on the fortress a mouse is in. There are many different regions inside the fort, each with their own culture and geographic quirks.

    After the Great battle for Izu in 936 AT, the Mordla Diamyo stripped the South and West walls of their precious metal lining and took it with them on their caravan to rebuild Morglordia.

    • Areas of Note

    Izu consists mainly of Edo, the Capital of Hojo. It is here where the NingyoMordla Legislation gathers to rule the country.

  • Edo - Edo contains many different geographical areas listed below

    • First Level

  • Gate - This is the primary entrance into Edo. It is protected by Guardmice loyal to the current Shogun.
  • Bar District - The most fluid of the regions of Edo, always changing families and businesses every few moons. Information can be found here for a price.
  • Craftmouse District - Contains a large varity of craftermice from across the Hojo Region and beyond. Fires are common in there area as the ground is soft and fluffy
  • Great Nest - This large fluffy structure has been the burrow for many a mouse in need of a place to raise their young. A large number of burrows have been dug into it throughout the years.
  • Market District - Contains the markets of legitamate mice. Ware of less craftworthy things are sold here such as food, insects, or other various goods.
  • Food Storage - Contains the food for the Izu region in large wood and metal grainaries.
  • The Great Climb - A large guarded hill that trainsitions to different levels of Edo.

    • Second Level

  • Living District 1,2,3 - Contain housing for various mice who live and work in or arround Edo
  • Academia - The largest colection of research and science based workers in Hojo. This area contains labs, schools, and guilds that deal with things from herbs, to insects.
  • GuardMice - Sleeping quarters and armory for the GuardMice of Edo.
  • The Great Aquifer - Contains Edo's water storage. A large lake that has a hole through the remain floor exists here and fills with water from rain. It is covered by the mice in winter.

    • Third Level

  • Arms - Contains all of the weapons and armor smiths and sellsmen. It is carefully watched by the Shogun to prevent arming the wrong people.
  • Audience Chamber - Contains the room the meetings take place in that are in utmost importance to the Country. The Hijo Council meets here every four mooons.
  • Hijo Daimyo Private District - Contains the Hijo Daimyo's houses, craftmice, guardsmice and heir. No entrance unless summoned.

    • Roof

  • Bee Stewards - BeeKeepers work here to collect and produce honey from bees. During times of seige, the bees are roused to attack attackers.
  • Terrance Farms - Farms that produce crops for the mice that live in Edo. Soil was carted up the Great Climb to produce these farms under the Hijo Shogunate.
  • Insect Range - Various Insects are raised up in the insect range.
