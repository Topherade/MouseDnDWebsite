    • Terrain

    The TenoHeiquiling region has farmlands near the Gonglu Road, with forests towards the east of it. To the north of the region is the Holy city of Kyo where no outsiders are permited. In the south of the region is Pengzi, a fort that rivils that of the capital of Edo. Although it is not as large, it has its many defenses built into it. To the East is Jia Shu, a city built into the stump of a downed tree.

    • Areas of Note

  • Kyo - The Holy Imperial City. It has been the home of the Tobo Family since 0 AT. No outsiders are allowed to enter into the city, but it is said that the city is on a spiritual hotspot that allows for great conduction of spiritual power.

  • Pengzi - A great fort that rivals the strength of Edo. Although it is not as big and only has one floor, Pengzi has hanging houses and bridges inside that make it difficult for attackers to reach and conquire it. There is an interior metal fort that the Weizhu control has has never fallen.

  • Jia Shu - The Tree Fort. Jai Shu was built into a down tree by the Genji family years past. The city is known for the centipedes and worms it raises as well as being a bustling trade center with the Bunnies of Yamashan.
