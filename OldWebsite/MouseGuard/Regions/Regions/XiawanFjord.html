Daimyo

  • Hijo

    • Terrain

    The Hijo rose to powerin the late 800's. Once they obtained significant support from other Daimyos, they fought the Genji Shogunate in 901 AT. Moving from Aokigahara they cut off the Genji supply line to Edo and sieged it out until they gain control of the Fort. They then seized the emperior in Kyo in 906 AT. The Genji being starved out on all sides after the Kirikabu of Cyprus Grove betrayed them in 907 surrendered unconditionally in 908 AT.
