    • Areas of Note

  • Doros - Capital city of the Changlu providence. It was razed 740 AT, by the Weishu clan during the Famu secession from the Weishu clan over religious issues. It was razed again in 932 AT by the Dreadwood Mercs, the Mordla, and the Wei Daimyos. It is built into the side of a small hill as it was orginally a mine. It is the former stronghold of the Famu clan. The Republic of Changlu's Tri-Camerial legislation is seated in Doros. Doros is known for their efficient factories that produce manufactured goods.

  • Heartbreaker Bog - The former seat of the Himitsu Diamyo, but was abandonned when they under took their grand pillgramage to Sabaku in 936 AT. The republic of Changlu gladdly accepted their people into their province and worked to restore the city. Heartbreaker Bog was built a top of a bog with boardwalks above the swamp between the various city centers. Its main industry is blacksmithing as well as the capture and slaughtering of insects for food produce.

  • Diku - The former seat of the Wain Daiymo before they moved north to the sea and took the name Ruorm. The city is built into the foundation of Izu and its large gate has been used for many years to protect the citizens of Izu in times of war. The city is known for the famous wine it produces as well as the black market goods that flow out of the city.

  • The Gonglu Road - The famous toll Road of the Famu. When they were overthrown, the Republic uses the tolls to taxes goods that go through the providence. These taxes are used to bolster industry in Doros and the other cities of Changlu.
